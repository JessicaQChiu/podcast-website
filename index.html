About the Blue Days Podcast

Blue days: when your day is going all wrong or devoid of joy. Everyone's had them, so let's talk about it together.

Welcome to the Blue Days Podcast, where we explore topics surrounding mental health with empathy, insight, and a commitment to fostering understanding. From anxiety and depression to self-care and resilience, we will discuss experiences and strategies to support individuals (and hopefully you!) on their mental health journey.

Whether you're seeking guidance, validation, or simply wanting to know you're not alone in all of this, listen in as we navigate the intricacies having those blue days. Together, let's shine a light on mental health and embrace the warmth of life.

Episodes

Ep.1 - Rain or Shine

What to do when the rain never stops?

Season 1 • 15 / 01 / 2024

In our first episode of our mental health podcast, we delve into the complex landscape of depression. We begin by defining depression, dispelling common myths, and the importance of seeking help. Through personal anecdotes and expert insights, we explore the nature of depression and acknowledge its diverse manifestations and the unique experiences of individuals grappling with it. We'll be discussing how to developing resilience when navigating the challenges of depression.

Ep.2 - Finding Calm in the Storm

Break out of that endless spiral of anxiety

Season 1 • 22 / 01 / 2024

From generalized anxiety to panic attacks, we'll dive right into it. We take a look at evidence-based techniques for coping with anxiety, emphasizing the importance of self-care, mindfulness, and seeking professional support when needed. It's time for us to guide you towards a path of finding peace within yourself.

Ep.3 - Whether the Weather Is or Is Not

A Guide to Developing Better Habits

Season 1 • 29 / 01 / 2024

Kicking bad habits and starting better ones is a journey of self-discovery and allowing life to be easier for yourself. It requires awareness, commitment, and a willingness to embrace change. We'll be talking about how by understanding our patterns underlying our habits, setting clear intentions, and cultivating healthier alternatives, we can liberate ourselves from the grip of negative behaviors and create space for growth.

Ep.4 - Enjoy the Sunshine

The links between physical and mental health

Season 1 • 05 / 02 / 2024

Engaging in regular physical activity has been shown to release endorphins, those feel-good hormones that can lift your mood, reduce stress, promote better sleep, and provide a healthy outlet for managing emotions. That means exercise isn't just about physical fitness - it's a powerful ally for your mental well-being too. We'll be talking about helping you feel more balanced, resilient, and empowered in your daily life through incorporating a little more attention to your physical health in this episode.

Ep.5 - Moving Forward to Tomorrow

Understanding and Coping with Grief and Loss

Season 1 • 12 / 02 / 2024

We embark on a journey about grief where we break down emotions and experiences that accompany loss. Whether you're grappling with your own grief or seeking to support a loved one in their time of need, listen in as we talk about the grieving process and how to cope through it. While grief may never fully disappear for some, it get easier to manage and can also evolve into a source of strength and meaning in one's life.
